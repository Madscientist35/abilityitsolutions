Epos Systems & Payments Integration

Custom Built Solutions

No business operates the same that is why we build custom solutions tailored to suit individual requirements.

Our Epos System is a connected solution that collects and exchanges data from a central cloud server to operate, manage and analyse transactions in real-time. It even works offline and updates when connection is restored.

Our software comes with a host of features & Integrations to help maximise your business sales & customer interaction experience. We have packages to suit all types of industry from bars, restraunts, cafes, salons, service & retail.

The different levels of software & features are listed below but we also have the following integrations available.

  • • Loyality Program
  • • Accounting
  • • Ecommerce
  • • The Delivery Guys
